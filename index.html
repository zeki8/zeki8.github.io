About me

Hi friends, my name is Maulana Dzaki Munawar, people usually call me kozek or dzael. I was born in a “rain city” in which my city always be the causes of the flood in the capital city, Jakarta. On the 7th February 2oo1, I was born in Bogor with a white skin. Even though I was born in Bogor, I spent my childhood in the East Java province. However, the good this is that I made many friends from those cities. After I had my journey in the East Java province, I lost my white skin and my skin color becomes black, totally black. Moreover, I am the second child of three siblings. My sister gets bored staying in Indonesia and she decided to be an international student at Xi’an Jiaotong-Liverpool University. My brother, his name is Luthfi Ghalib, this handsome guy and the slave of his girlfriend is sort of difficult to study. He plays online game every night till I get bored seeing him. .
About my education? I used to move my school to many cities in which the schools were the most favorite school in the city. However, after many years going for school in East Java province, I felt a little disguise of my childhood going to public schools, and when I moved to Bogor, my mom’s friends told me that there is one good Islamic school named Insantama. To be honest, I really had no idea about the school especially the school is called Islamic school. I was wondering to myself, what is that school? It turns out that in that school, I had so many friends and I seldom study haha. I mean, unlike public schools with the teachers explain the course and students finish assignments (the bored public schools], however, this school is not only studying, but the teachers let us to play hide and seek, football and cards at the school, even we played with the teachers. After I was graduated from the school, I continued my junior high school to the same school with different level. Though the name is the same, the system of study is basically different. I was focused to attitude and religion beliefs there.
Alhamdulillah, praise to Allah, after the news I heard from my sister that I was accepted to this school, I feel proud. I feel that I am way smarter than my friends. In the first year, I was starting to get to know my friends, teachers, and security guards. The first friend I know when the orientation of this school was Fajar Danu and the first friend I know in 1-4 is Akif Azmi. Being friends with them is like being the friend of the cleverest students. During our study time, I struggled a lot with the course and practical laboratory, started from the assignments, mid-term until final exams. I started to feel that this school makes me busy all the time. In the next 11th grade, this pain has not finished yet. In the 1oth grade, we were faced with the gravimetric laboratory. In the 11th grade, we are faced with the volumetric, microbiology and analysis instrument laboratory, meaning that I have to write 3 reports in a week. The friends in my 11th grade are the people I have not known before. In this 11th grade, the 1st friend I know is ricko and the most mischievous friend is hizkia.

development of science and technology

One thing interests me the most is the knowledge of technology called “IPTEK (Ilmu Pengetahuan dan Teknologi]”. The word IPTEK is related to the understanding of research, discovering something new based on the experience we have, and technology as a tool for human to provide their needs or ease their jobs. I think, all those things are able to create new discoveries. When I was in primary school, an android phone did not exist yet, however, primary school students recently are playing with their android phone. We will never be able to get rid of science and technology, but we must adapt in to it.
